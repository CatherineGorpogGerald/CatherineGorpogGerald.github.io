About SheaShine

Shea Shine is a Ghanaian owned shea butter processing company that seeks to be become the leading provider of quality unrefined shea butter and bulk black soap. We want to build a business that will be listed amongst the top shea butter processing brands in the world. This will be accomplished by providing these products at fair prices while meeting and exceeding customer's expectations.

The Company is based in Tamale in northern Ghana, a region with limited economic opportunities. Shea Shine has a dual objective of producing high quality products such as Ivory butter, yellow butter and bulk black soap in any quantity, while improving the economic status of rural women through innovative and sustainable employment and education.

We seek to be a global supplier of natural and affordable shea butter and shea-based bath product sourced through an ethical and environmentally friendly supply chain.

Shea shine currently have three products we supply, namely conventional butter, black soap and conventional yellow butter but with time we will upscale into more products

Ivory Shea Butter

Shea butter is a fat extracted from the nut of the African Shea tree (Vitellaria paradoxa). Widely used in cosmetics production as a moisturizer, salve or lotion. Shea butter is edible and is used in food preparation as well. Shea butter has also gained a lot of recognition in the cocoa industry and used as Cocoa Butter Equivalents.

Our product is unrefined and well packaged to prevent contamination and tampering. Our product is always fresh and made from the most recent harvests of Shea nuts to ensure you get the best quality Shea butter. The colour may vary from off white to Ivory. For yellow butter, the colour is deep yellow as a result of the addition of natural root extracts. The colour largely depends of the processing and also the raw material (shea nuts)

We supply in bulk both locally and internationally. Product Packaging: Poly ethylene liners in a 5x5 ply carton

Black Soap

It is handmade in Ghana, West Africa and we buy directly from the source. We ensure that value addition at the source is maximized: this black soap provides jobs for women in poor rural areas of Northern Ghana.

Our soap is supplied in bulk packaging. It consists of compressed soap noodles, but is not yet compact and can be used for further processing. 100% natural raw african black soap made of natural ingredients: 50% shea butter, 25% coconut oil and 25% palm kernel oil. This is unrefined.

The color of this African black soap may vary from light brown to dark brown and it is softer than a typical bar of soap. The color of the soap is determined by the production process; the longer it is heated during the cooking process, the darker the black soap. The moisture content in the soap determines the hardness; the drier the harder.

Yellow Butter

Yellow Shea Butter is pure unrefined shea butter with one additional ingredient, the extract of the African Borututu Tree, which has been used by African natives for centuries to help with healing and soothing the skin. The inside of this tree is a deep yellow colour and this is how our Yellow Shea Butter gets its colour. This butter does not contain palm oil or other ingredients. Yellow Shea Butter is nutrient rich, packed with vitamins and antioxidants. It is effective in deeply moisturizing the skin and to help repair severely dry or cracked skin.
